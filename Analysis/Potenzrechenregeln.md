#mathe-brueckenkurs

1. $b^1=b$
2. $b^0=1$
3. $b^{-a}=\frac{1}{b^{a}}$
4. $b^c*b^d = b^{c+d}$
5. $b^c:b^d=b^{c-d}$
6. $a^c*b^c=(ab)^c$
7. $a^c:b^c=(a:b)^c$
8. $rb^c \pm sb^c =(r\pm s)b^c$
9. $(b^c)^d=b^{c*d}$
10. $\sqrt[d]{b^c}=b^{\frac c d}$
11. $b^{-(\frac 1 d)}=\frac 1 {\sqrt[d] b}$
12. $b^{-(\frac c d)}=\frac 1 {\sqrt[d] {b^c}}$
13. $(\frac a b )^{-c}=(\frac ba)^c$
